All Flings Considered is a show about sex, relationships, and awkward Tinder dates. Every Monday from 4-4:30 tune into KUCI 88.9FM to hear 30 minutes of non-intimate conversation on some intimate topic related to millenial hookup culture. It's pillow talk for public radio.

Design at UCI is an organization for designers and creative individuals of all skill levels and backgrounds at UCI. Through education, collaboration, and innovation, Design at UCI strives to be a space to encourage students to make their campus beautiful.
